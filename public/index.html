ABOUT US

Thai-Style Beach Living

JaiDee Resort (ใจดี รีสอร์ท) is a traditional Thai-style beachfront resort located on the pristine shores of Sichon, Nakhon Si Thammarat. Our name means "Good Heart" - reflecting the warmth and genuine hospitality we bring to every guest.

With traditional wooden bungalows, an infinity pool overlooking the sea, and a seafood restaurant serving the freshest catch daily, JaiDee Resort is the perfect escape from the city.
